How to Make a Cheesecake

tarte portion:

  • butter - 60g
  • egg yolk - 1
  • sugar - 40g
  • flour - 120g

preheat oven to 350 F put in food processor in a cake pan, press the mix to create a crust bake at 350 F for 10 min

filling portion:

  • heavy cream - 1 cup
  • 2 eggs
  • cream cheese - 1 pack
  • flour - 3 TBSP
  • sugar - 2/3 cup
  • lemon juice - 1TBSP

put in food processor pour onto crust bake at 350 F for about 30 min. time will depend on oven strength.
